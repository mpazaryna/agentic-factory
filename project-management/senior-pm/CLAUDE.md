# Senior PM

Portfolio management, quantitative risk analysis, resource capacity planning, and executive reporting. Includes Python analysis scripts and reference templates.

## Commands
- **senior-pm** — Entry point for portfolio health, risk, capacity, and reporting tasks

## Skills
- **senior-pm** — Portfolio management expertise: health scoring, risk quantification (EMV, Monte Carlo), resource capacity planning, prioritization frameworks (WSJF, RICE, ICE), executive reporting

## Scripts
- `project_health_dashboard.py` — Multi-dimensional health scoring with RAG status
- `risk_matrix_analyzer.py` — Risk quantification, EMV analysis, mitigation strategies
- `resource_capacity_planner.py` — Utilization modeling and what-if scenarios

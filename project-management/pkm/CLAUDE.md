# PKM

Personal knowledge management rituals — daily capture, weekly/monthly reviews, and planning.

## Skills

### Daily Rituals
- **kickoff** — Morning orientation: carry-overs, project status, daily focus
- **interstitial** — Quick timestamped note capture throughout the day
- **shutdown** — End-of-day closure: accomplishments, blockers, tomorrow's frog

### Weekly
- **weekly-plan** — Start-of-week planning with load calculation and time blocking
- **weekly-review** — Consolidated view of the past week with Clockify data
- **weekly-summary** — In-depth weekly retrospective from daily notes and metrics

### Monthly
- **monthly-summary** — Comprehensive monthly retrospective across all data sources

### Utilities
- **remarkable** — Extract dated entries from Remarkable PDF notebooks into interstitial notes
